Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, focusing on social-emotional development, literacy, and numeracy skills in a nurturing and play-based environment.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th grade, emphasizing a balanced approach to academics, arts, and physical education.

📚

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program caters to students from 5th to 8th grade, providing a rigorous and engaging curriculum that prepares students for high school and beyond.

🔬

STEM

Our STEM program offers hands-on learning experiences in science, technology, engineering, and math, encouraging students to explore and develop their passions in these fields.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program provides a comprehensive education in music, visual arts, drama, and dance, helping students develop their creative expression and appreciation for the arts.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers competitive sports teams for students in grades 5-8, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
