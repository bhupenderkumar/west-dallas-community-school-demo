Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program prepares young children for a lifelong love of learning, with a focus on play-based education and social-emotional development.

📚

Elementary

In our elementary program, students engage in a rigorous and engaging curriculum that emphasizes literacy, mathematics, science, and social studies.

📝

Middle School

Our middle school program builds on the foundation established in elementary school, with a focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills.

🚀

Upper School

Our upper school program prepares students for high school and beyond, with a focus on advanced coursework, leadership development, and community engagement.

🔬

STEM Program

Our STEM program offers students a rigorous and engaging curriculum in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, with hands-on projects and real-world applications.

🎨

Arts Program

Our arts program provides students with opportunities to explore and develop their creative talents in music, visual arts, drama, and dance.

🏈

Athletics Program

Our athletics program offers students a chance to develop teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness skills through competitive sports and recreation activities.
