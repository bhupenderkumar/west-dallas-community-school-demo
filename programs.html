Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, with a focus on play-based learning, socialization, and emotional development. Our experienced teachers use a child-centered approach to foster a love of learning and prepare students for a successful transition to elementary school.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th grade, with a focus on literacy, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes and experienced teachers ensure that each child receives personalized attention and support to excel academically.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs serve students from 5th to 8th grade, with a focus on academic rigor, character development, and extracurricular activities. Our teachers use a project-based approach to engage students in hands-on learning experiences that foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

🔬

STEM

Our STEM program is designed for students who are passionate about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Our experienced teachers use hands-on activities and real-world applications to engage students in learning and prepare them for a successful career in these fields.

🎭

Arts

Our arts program is designed to foster creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for the arts. Our experienced teachers provide instruction in music, visual arts, and drama, with a focus on building confidence, teamwork, and leadership skills.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our athletic program is designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. Our experienced coaches provide instruction in a variety of sports, with a focus on building confidence, discipline, and leadership skills.
