Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, with a focus on play-based learning, socialization, and emotional development. Our experienced teachers use a child-centered approach to foster a love of learning and prepare students for a successful transition to elementary school.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th grade, with a focus on literacy, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our small class sizes and experienced teachers ensure that each child receives personalized attention and support to excel academically.

📊

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs serve students from 5th to 8th grade, with a focus on academic rigor, character development, and extracurricular activities. Our teachers use a project-based approach to engage students in hands-on learning experiences that foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
