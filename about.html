Our Mission & History

West Dallas Community School was founded in 2001 with the vision of providing a high-quality education to the underserved community of West Dallas. Our school has grown from a small group of dedicated teachers to a thriving community of over 250 students, with a diverse student body that reflects the richness of our city.

At West Dallas Community School, we believe in teaching the whole child - academically, socially, and emotionally. Our experienced faculty uses research-based methods to engage students in hands-on learning experiences that foster critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

In addition to academic rigor, we also prioritize character development and extracurricular activities that promote social responsibility, teamwork, and leadership. Our students participate in clubs, sports teams, and community service projects that help them develop essential life skills and build strong relationships with their peers and teachers.
