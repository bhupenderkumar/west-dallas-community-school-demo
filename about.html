Our Mission & History

West Dallas Community School was founded in 2001 with a mission to provide a high-quality, inclusive education to students in the heart of Dallas. Our school has grown from a small group of students to a thriving community of over 250 students.

Our teaching approach is centered around project-based learning, where students engage in hands-on activities and real-world applications to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills.

At West Dallas Community School, we believe that a well-rounded education extends beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular programs and character development initiatives help students build confidence, self-discipline, and a strong sense of community.
